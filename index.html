Team history

WetellRacing is a team of two people, Felix Wetell and Christer Wetell. The team started in 2014, with the purchase of the first bike, an Aprilia RS125 year model 2000. This was the beginning of the future racing career for Felix. Felix used the Aprilia for training during the year of 2015, getting a total of around 20 hours of riding. At the end of the season he raced in the Linköpings club championship finishing last. The point was not to win, but to gain race experince, track time and improve the riding. Late 2015, the Aprilia was up for sale and the search for a 600cc bike began. After a cople of months of research an Yamaha R6R 2006 was found for sale. It meet the requirment for a proper race bike
